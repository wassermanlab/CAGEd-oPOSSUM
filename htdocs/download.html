Databases

The CAGEd-oPOSSUM databases may be downloaded directly from our database servers using the mysldump facility.

There are two CAGEd-oPOSSUM databases, one for each species:

CAGEd_oPOSSUM_human
CAGEd_oPOSSUM_mouse

The databases are hosted on cagedop.cmmt.ubc.ca and may be accessed using MySQL username 'cagedop_r'.
It may be necessary to use the --skip-lock-tables option to mysqldump, e.g.:

$ mysqldump -h cagedop.cmmt.ubc.ca -u cagedop_r --skip-lock-tables CAGEd_oPOSSUM_human > CAGEd_oPOSSUM_human.sql
